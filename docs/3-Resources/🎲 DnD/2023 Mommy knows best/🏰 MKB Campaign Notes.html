General Lore

Xhorhas Nation and Kryn Dynasty

  • The nation is ruled by the Kryn Dynasty
    • current leader Leylas Kryn the Nation and City Leader
  • The Xhorhas nation just got Jiglow City connected to international trade via Radiant Citadel City. They were previously isolated but boast open values, equality for races and technological advancement. The connection is a meaningful political move.

History of Radiant Citadel City and the Amulet

  • found clue on Tournament celebrating the Trade Route Establishment during the search for the lost Amulet, underwater where we met Lula the Rescue from Underwater Caves
  • The amulet makes people see suffering and dispair of the founder of the Radiant Citadel City, as well as a strong connecting feeling to the city, plus loneliness.
  • we are about to find out what happens next

Tournament celebrating the Trade Route Establishment

Lore

  • As part of the festivities surrounding the newly established trade route to the Xhorhas Nation and Kryn Dynasty via the Cities Radiant Citadel City (hub) and Jiglow City (new trade partner), there is a tournament.
    • Leylas Kryn the Nation and City Leader
    • Sholeh the City Co-Leader
  • As this is such a big deal, there is big prize money, which is sought after by many travelers hoping to make in Radiant Citadel City and the area around.
  • Our party meets here, forms a pact for this one tournament to compete and win.
    • Kilidh by Rhage has been travelling for a while and cannot afford even a place to sleep in this big city, is thus looking for money.
    • Selisa Agetul by Meike has come here to help people, and was guided to the tournament as an introduction the city and it's people, noticing the party needs help and feels this her calling.
    • Juniper Newt by Mia hopes the gold will get her into The Dragon Tit Inn as a performer, as she is struggling to make money as a relatively unsuccessful entertainer
    • Veit Ungard by Kanto is here cause he be travellin
  • We meet Ayo and her party there for the first time
  • Saved Lula the Rescue from Underwater Caves and found out about the History of Radiant Citadel City and the Amulet
