Background

Character Overview

  • Race: Shifter (Catgirl) - Can shift into a full cat form for up to 1 minute.
  • Class: Bard / School of Lore - Values truth, knowledge, and ethics.
    • Alignment: Chaotic Good.
    • Hails from a high-tech hometown with a solar punk or "green steampunk" aesthetic.
  • Primary Attributes: Charisma and Dexterity.
    • Proficient in persuasion, insight, deception, stealth, and performance.

Backstory

  • Uncertain Parentage:

    • Speculation that parents could be either human + tabaxi or human + cat god, or possibly tied to a deity.
    • No concrete knowledge about biological parents, only best guesses.

  • Community Upbringing:

    • Grew up in a community that reveres cats as god-like beings (similar to cows in India).
    • Discovered ability to shift into the god-like cat form as a child.
    • Learned to exploit this ability for personal gain, using persuasion and deception.
    • Initially used it to obtain attention, food, and other needs.
    • Kept identity as the shifter secret until teenage years when the ruse was discovered.

  • Forced to Leave Home:

    • People eventually uncovered the deception, leading to a need to flee.
    • Reason for traveling is to escape the consequences and seek a new sense of belonging.
    • Character is very community-oriented but lost their original sense of belonging.

  • Insecurities and Overcompensation:

    • Desperate to be part of a group, leading to overcompensation and people-pleasing behaviors.
    • Insecurity is masked by performing and being nice to others.
    • Character flaw: Vulnerability to temptation for true belonging.

  • Entertaining Abilities:

    • Skilled at entertaining, but not exceptionally so.
    • Craves contributing to a group or commune, seeking a sense of belonging.
    • Not motivated by personal fame or recognition.

Community Background

  • Collective Ethos:

    • Strong sense of collectivism, selflessness, and serving the group's needs.
    • Possibly descendants of former slaves or runaways with a focus on doing good.
    • Emphasis on strong ethics.

  • Isolation and Lack of Awareness:

    • Community is self-contained and isolated from other places, religions, and global politics.
    • Limited knowledge about the outside world, resulting in occasional clumsiness.
    • Unfamiliarity with customs and interaction rules.

  • Reserved Nature:

    • Character is somewhat reserved despite acting open and seeking attention.
    • Hides true self to some extent to cope with the pain of not belonging.

  • Technological Solarpunk Utopia:

    • Community combines medieval solarpunk aesthetics with magic and nature as a science.
    • Magic being normal and taught from early on
    • Unique technology designs: e.g., water purification using a miniature forest, bamboo pipes for flowing water.
    • Proficient in dealing with plants, nature, and animals.

Arrival in the Starting City

  • Journey and Current Status:

    • Ran away from the community and has been traveling.
    • Driven by a strong desire for belonging and seeking a larger community.
    • Stranded in the starting city and working as an entertainer to make a living.
    • Still actively searching for a cause and commune to join during free time.

  • Lack of Recognition and Wealth:

    • Character is not well-known and unlikely to be recognized.
    • Financially limited due to the entertainer lifestyle.
    • Not genuinely enthusiastic about performing.

Magic and Interactions

  • Magic Style:
    • Performs magic through words and touch, using systematic techniques and tools from nature.
    • Learns and reflects through meditation in nature and engineering-like books.
    • Casts spells by touching and communicating with others, utilizing persuasion, insight, and deception to inspire, heal, or hypnotize.

Roleplay ideas due to the background:

Generally:

  • She is desperate for community, will cling onto every chance to keep the band together.
  • Tries to hide her shifting from others as long as possible (eg. using distractions if others might watch)
  • Moments of wonder as she is unfamiliar with many of the customs
  • fascinated by or more knowledgeable about certain technologies or natural phenomena (due to solar punk background)

During fights:

  • doesnt want to hurt anyone, especially animals; always tries diplomacy first
  • uses persuasion, distractions and aid to fight/support first

She discovered she could do this as a kid, others couldn't at all. She doesn't know yet how or why.
