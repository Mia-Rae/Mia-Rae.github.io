The lost Amulet from the Underwater Caves

We walk back to the big tent with stage and get ready to present the medals.
After some time guests arrive, Leylas Kryn, the nations leader and Elder Ushru are on stage. We are asked to join them. There is a suprise quest: We have to get to the boats soon, but still have an hour. And don't know what to expect. We have an hour to prepare to head off.

Selisa wants to mingle in the crowds. Kilidh wants to check the stores. In the end we decide to rest at the boats. Where we meet the

We all get to the boat eventually and head off. It's a tini tiny island.
Elder Ushru puts up 12 Bottles on a table again. We are told we may challenge the other team. But if we do then we can get 200 gold instead of 100 gold per Person. We will also get a underwater-breathing potion that lasts 1h. The entire challenge will be underwater.
Juniper talks to Ayos team and makes a promise we will save each other to make sure we will not drown. They agree and we try and exchange knowledge.
We drink the potions and wait to get the info. Elder Ushru explains: we gotta retrieve a Amulet from the depths. There may only be one winner.

It begins: one cave is leading underwater. There is no natural light. We go in. Selisa creates light with her magic for us. Veit says to trust him lead the way. We do.

We dive in. Down into a 20ft deep cove with dense, very dense kelp forest blocking most of our vision, we cannot see the bottom. Veit tries locate but cant find it. So we split up and explore the first cave, find the way to move on. We come to a Y split, we continue south, passing a door.
We are now 40ft down, but we can see a crack with light beams entering the water. Other side is collapsed and inaccessible. We continue on, but the walls collapse further, stray rocks hit Selisa and Veit. They seem to be fine. We find a way through those loose rocks. And find a potion of healing, scroll of thunderwave lying under them.
We move on to next cavern, deep, deep down. Find a white pillar with lil piranha fishies, that seem to be attracted to any movement. We try and distract, but they notice Selisa and Veit, attacking them. We kill them with minimal damage with Kilidh holding onto Juniper's paw, as he's blind.

Continuing on we find a female halfling in the water that is unaware of us. Kilidh goes to check it out. She seems almost dead, but alive. There is sumthing dark behind the column behind her. We try and make her notice us. She points at the column, a severed human arm is floating in the water.
We try and move her. A shark appears. Selisa tells her to move up to the air pocket. While we fight.
Selisa hits the shark, doing massive damage and angering it. It comes for her, hits. She drops unconscious. We continue fighting while trying to save her. Soon, Veit revivers her, tho shes still pretty hurt. She lives!!
We continue fighting. Juniper throws (now very hurt) Veit a potion of healing, which he uses promptly as well as healing himself, too. He's tanking. After a v long and scary fight we finally kill it. Halfway through the halfling started helping us eventually, she seems freindly. As it's dying, the shark swims into the pillar, collapsing part of the structure, revealing another cave entrance.

While Juniper go look after the halfling, the other three venture forth. They find the Amulet around the sharks neck. Veit opens up the shark in search of meat but instead discovers very peculiar crystals that are crumbling. He takes the Amulet.
Moving on, they discover a forest after the hole with a fruit tree and waterfall. It seems like an exit.
They return to look after the origin of the shark, finding a half-eaten body, similar clothing as the halfling.

Meanwhile Juniper is with the halfling. She's freaking out, wanting to exit as fast as possible. She has a map for the exit. I cant convince her. So I follow her through the hole, meeting the rest of the party currently coming back to us.

We together make it through the hole again and find our way out. There is waterfalls and (seemingly religious) statue. The halfling asks for the Amulet and puts it around the neck of the statue. Selisa starts praying as it's her god.
